Reserved Instances help cut down your computing costs. You pay a low, one-time fee, then a disconted hourly rate so that you know what you're paying and that you're instance is available when you need it. Reserved instances are available for terms of 12 or 36 months, and when your term is up, there will be no interruption to your compute instance, the price will just be adjusted to the current on-demand price. Note: Additional taxes may apply.
